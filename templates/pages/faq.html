FAQ
How does the AI detect breast cancer from ultrasound images? ▶
It analyzes ultrasound images using AI models to detect if cancer is present or not. If cancer is detected, it identifies the type (e.g., benign or malignant) and provides a brief recommendation for further medical consultation.
What is a Breast Cancer Detection Tool? ▶
A web app that uses AI to analyze ultrasound images for early breast cancer detection.
Who should use this application? ▶
This web application is intended for medical professionals and patients seeking early detection of breast cancer.
Does this tool replace the Doctor? ▶
No, the tool assists doctors by providing additional insights but does not replace professional medical evaluation.
Is my data safe and private? ▶
Yes, your personal information is stored securely and used only for diagnostic purposes.
